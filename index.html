How It Works

  1. Contact me with your budget + needs (games, apps, target FPS, etc.)
  2. I recommend compatible parts + pricing and answer questions
  3. You approve the build and we lock in the part list
  4. Payment is required before ordering parts
  5. I assemble + stress-test and send photos/test results
  6. I ship with tracking (or arrange local pickup)

Shipping (Across North America)

I can ship PCs across North America. Every system is packed to reduce shipping damage risk and includes tracking. Shipping cost depends on your location and case size.

How I ship safely

  • GPU removed and packed separately (most important step)
  • Internal protection added to support components
  • Double-box packaging with padding on all sides
  • Photos taken before shipment + tracking number provided

Typical timeline: Build time 5–10 business days + shipping 4–7 business days.

Pricing

Pricing depends on the parts you choose. My build fee covers assembly, cable management, BIOS setup, driver installs, and stress testing.

  • Build Fee: $150–$250 (depending on complexity)
  • Shipping: customer pays (quoted before you approve)
  • Packaging: included

I don’t compete by cutting corners — I focus on build quality, honest part choices, and support.

Policies

Damage on arrival

If your shipment arrives damaged, contact me within 24 hours of delivery and send photos of:
